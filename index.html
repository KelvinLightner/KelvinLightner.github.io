About Me

I was born and raised in Lancaster, CA, a little town an hour away from Los Angeles. I come from a small family of four: my mom, dad, sister and I. I am a proud uncle to one niece and two nephews. My hope is to see my niece and nephews grow into strong, intelligent, and loving people. I am proud to say that they are well on their way.

My family is my primary motivation behind the things I hope to accomplish. I have been given the responsibility, by them, to pave the way. I have and will continue to do just that. In education, in career, and in whole self.

Beauty in the Struggle

"We may encounter many defeats but we must not be defeated." -Maya Angelou

From a very young age, I was taught to never be afraid to challenge myself and to never shy away from working hard. In light of this, the difficult homework questions, leadership opportunities, and personal growth battles always had a larger purpose. They would produce in me the refining qualities of maturity.

From high school, to U.C. Berkeley, to becoming a Software Engineer, my greatest challenges have produced in me my greatest strengths, all of which, are still leading me to become a man of dignity, empathy and intelligence.
